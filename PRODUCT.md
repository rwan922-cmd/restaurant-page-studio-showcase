# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

New Zealand software recruiters, engineering managers, student technology
clubs and potential employers evaluating Ruiqiao Wang for lawful part-time
employee, internship or graduate-track opportunities.

## Product Purpose

Restaurant Page Studio is Ruiqiao's flagship full-stack portfolio case study.
It demonstrates how a bilingual customer experience, searchable visual menu,
merchant-confirmed reservation workflow and protected merchant workspace can
be designed, implemented, tested and deployed as one coherent product.

The homepage and resume default to English for recruiters and provide an
explicit Chinese switch. Restaurant demonstrations remain bilingual because
multilingual product communication is part of the engineering evidence.

This is not a commercial offer. It does not accept restaurant orders, quote
prices or claim merchant authorisation.

## Success Criteria

- A recruiter can identify Ruiqiao, his degree, graduation date and work
  eligibility without searching.
- The case study explains the problem, architecture, technical decisions,
  verification and limits of the demonstration.
- The live product proves responsive frontend, serverless backend, validation,
  privacy and accessibility skills.
- AI assistance is disclosed without overstating independent expertise.
- Private source files and commercial contact drafts never appear in public
  portfolio surfaces.

## Brand Personality

Direct, thoughtful and technically specific.

The voice should sound like a capable first-year student who can explain what
he built and what he is still learning. Avoid inflated seniority, generic AI
claims and startup marketing language.

## Anti-references

- Generic Codex or AI portfolio templates.
- Cream paper backgrounds, Georgia display headings and diffuse gradients.
- Skill-percentage bars, floating technology logos and empty metric counters.
- Identical card grids, fake testimonials and invented client outcomes.
- Claims of client work, commercial availability or production adoption.
- Visual imitation of any restaurant brand.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Support keyboard navigation, visible focus, 200% zoom,
reduced motion, descriptive bilingual alternative text, 44px touch targets
and readable text on small screens.
